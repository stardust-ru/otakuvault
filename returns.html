Returns & Refunds

Our hassle-free return policy ensures your complete satisfaction with every purchase.

30-Day Return Policy

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund or exchange.

Return Process

1. Request a Return

Contact us within 30 days of receiving your order. Provide your order number and reason for return.

2. Get Return Authorization

We'll review your request and provide a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number within 24-48 hours.

3. Package Your Return

Pack the item securely in its original packaging with all accessories and documentation included.

4. Ship It Back

Use a trackable shipping method and include the RMA number on the package. We'll provide the return address.

5. Receive Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following conditions:

  • Time Limit: Returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Condition: Unopened and in original packaging (for full refund)
  • Opened Items: May be subject to a 15-30% restocking fee
  • Documentation: Include all original accessories, manuals, and inserts
  • Damage: Items damaged due to mishandling are not eligible

Refund Information

Refund Methods

  • Original Payment: Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Store Credit: Available immediately for exchanges or future purchases
  • Processing Time: 5-7 business days after we receive your return
  • Bank Processing: Additional 3-5 business days for credit cards

Non-Refundable Items

  • Downloadable digital products
  • Gift cards
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Items damaged due to customer mishandling

Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged or defective:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Take photos of the damaged item and packaging
  • We will provide a prepaid return label
  • Choose between a replacement or full refund
  • No restocking fees for damaged/defective items

Exchanges

Want to exchange for a different item?

  • Follow the same return process
  • Note your preferred exchange item in the return request
  • We'll process the exchange once we receive your return
  • Price differences will be charged or refunded accordingly

Contact Us

Have questions about your return?

  • Email: returns@otakuvault.com
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours
  • Include: Order number and reason for return
