Product Questions

What does "1/8 scale" mean?

Scale figures are sized relative to the character's "actual" height:

  • 1/8 scale: Figure is 1/8th the size of the character (typically 8-9 inches tall)
  • 1/7 scale: Slightly larger, about 9-10 inches
  • 1/6 scale: Larger format, 10-12 inches
  • 1/4 scale: Premium large format, 16-18 inches
  • Nendoroid/Chibi: Stylized cute versions, usually 4 inches

Scale is important for collectors who want consistent sizing across their display.

What's the difference between PVC and ABS figures?

Most figures use a combination of materials:

  • PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride): Soft, flexible plastic used for main body parts. Allows for detailed sculpting and painting.
  • ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene): Hard, rigid plastic used for bases, weapons, and structural parts. More durable.

High-end figures may also include die-cast metal parts for weight and premium feel.

How should I care for my figures?

Protect your investment with proper care:

  • Display: Keep away from direct sunlight to prevent fading
  • Dusting: Use soft microfiber cloth or air duster weekly
  • Cleaning: Damp cloth with mild soap for stubborn dirt
  • Temperature: Avoid extreme heat (above 85°F/30°C) and humidity
  • Handling: Wash hands before touching; avoid touching painted surfaces
  • Storage: Keep original boxes for potential future moves or sales
