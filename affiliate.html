Amazon Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency about our partnerships and how we earn revenue.

Federal Trade Commission (FTC) Disclosure

OtakuVault is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

What This Means

When you click on product links on our website and make a purchase on Amazon, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. This affiliate relationship helps us:

  • Maintain and improve our website
  • Curate quality product selections
  • Provide detailed product information and reviews
  • Offer student discounts and special promotions
  • Create helpful content for the anime community

Important: You pay the same price whether you use our links or go directly to Amazon. Our commission comes from Amazon's side of the transaction, not from you.

How It Works

1. Product Links

Most product links on our website are affiliate links. When you click these links, a small tracking cookie is placed on your browser. This cookie tells Amazon that you came from OtakuVault.

2. Your Purchase

If you make a purchase on Amazon within 24 hours of clicking our link (or add an item to your cart within 24 hours and complete the purchase within 89 days), we earn a commission.

3. Commission Rates

Commission rates vary by product category, typically ranging from 1% to 10% of the purchase price. These rates are set by Amazon and change periodically.

4. Full Cart Commission

We earn commission on your entire cart, not just the specific product you clicked. If you click our link for a figure but also buy a book and headphones, we earn commission on all items.

Our Commitment to You

Our affiliate relationship with Amazon does not influence our product recommendations. We are committed to:

  • Honest Reviews: We only recommend products we genuinely believe in
  • Fair Pricing: We highlight the best deals, regardless of commission rate
  • Transparency: We clearly disclose our affiliate relationships
  • Quality First: We prioritize product quality over commission potential
  • User Experience: Your trust is more valuable than any commission
