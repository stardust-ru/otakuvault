Shipping Information

Everything you need to know about delivery options, timeframes, and tracking your anime collectibles.

Important Notice

All orders are fulfilled by our partner retailers (primarily Amazon). Shipping policies, rates, and delivery times are determined by the specific retailer and carrier. This page provides general guidance based on standard Amazon shipping practices.

Shipping Methods & Timeframes

Amazon Prime

1-2 Business Days

Free for Prime members on eligible items. Includes tracking and guaranteed delivery.

Standard Shipping

5-8 Business Days

Free on orders over $25. Economical option with full tracking capabilities.

Expedited Shipping

2-3 Business Days

Calculated at checkout based on weight and destination. Faster than standard.

International

7-21 Business Days

Varies by country. Import duties and taxes may apply. Tracking included.

Shipping Destinations

Domestic Shipping (United States)

We ship to all 50 states, including:

  • Continental United States (all 48 states)
  • Alaska and Hawaii (may take additional 2-3 days)
  • P.O. Boxes and APO/FPO addresses (via USPS)
  • U.S. Territories: Puerto Rico, Guam, U.S. Virgin Islands

International Shipping

Available to over 100 countries including:

  • North America: Canada, Mexico
  • Europe: UK, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Netherlands, and more
  • Asia-Pacific: Japan, Australia, South Korea, Singapore
  • Other: Brazil, UAE, select others

International customers are responsible for all import duties, taxes, and customs fees. These charges are not included in the product price or shipping cost.

Order Tracking

Track your anime collectibles every step of the way:

Order Confirmation

Receive email confirmation with order details and estimated delivery date immediately after purchase.

Shipped

Receive tracking number via email once your package leaves the fulfillment center. Track via carrier website or Amazon.

In Transit

Monitor progress through sorting facilities and transit hubs. Updates typically every 24 hours.

Out for Delivery

Package is on the delivery vehicle and will arrive by end of day. Signature may be required for high-value items.

Delivered

Package delivered! Photo confirmation may be available. Check safe drop locations if not at door.

Tracking Your Order

Visit Amazon Order History or use the tracking number provided in your shipping confirmation email. You can also sign up for text alerts for real-time updates.

Packaging & Handling

We understand that anime collectibles are precious. Our partner retailers use specialized packaging:

  • Protective Materials: Bubble wrap, air pillows, and foam inserts
  • Sturdy Boxes: Double-walled corrugated cardboard for figures
  • Fragile Labels: Packages marked as fragile for careful handling
  • Original Packaging: Items shipped in original manufacturer boxes when possible
  • Collector's Grade: Special handling for limited editions and high-value items

All packages are insured against damage during transit. In the rare event of shipping damage, please contact us immediately with photos.

Shipping Costs

Free Shipping Thresholds

  • Amazon Prime Members: Free 1-2 day shipping on eligible items
  • Standard Shipping: Free on orders $25+ (non-Prime)
  • International: Free shipping available on select items over $49

Calculated Shipping

For orders below free shipping thresholds or expedited options:

  • Based on weight, dimensions, and destination
  • Displayed at checkout before payment
  • Multiple items may ship separately to minimize cost

Delivery Issues

Lost Packages

If your tracking shows delivered but you haven't received your package:

  • Check with neighbors and household members
  • Look in safe drop locations (porch, garage, leasing office)
  • Wait 24 hours (sometimes marked delivered early)
  • Contact carrier directly with tracking number
  • File a claim if not located within 48 hours

Damaged Packages

If your collectible arrives damaged:

  • Take photos of the damaged packaging and item immediately
  • Keep all packaging materials for inspection
  • Contact Amazon Customer Service within 48 hours
  • Request replacement or refund
  • Report to us at support@otakuvault.com for follow-up

Address Changes

Once an order is placed, the shipping address cannot be changed. Please double-check your address before completing your purchase. If you made an error, contact Amazon immediately - they may be able to cancel the order so you can reorder with the correct address.

Processing Times

Most orders are processed within 24 hours:

  • In-stock items: Ship within 1 business day
  • Pre-orders: Ship on or after release date
  • Limited editions: May require 2-3 days for special handling
  • Weekend orders: Processed next business day

During peak seasons (holidays, major anime conventions), processing may take 2-3 business days.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship to P.O. boxes?

Yes, via USPS. However, large figures may not fit in standard P.O. boxes and will need to be picked up at the post office.

Can I request signature confirmation?

Signature confirmation is automatically applied to orders over $500. For other orders, you can request it through Amazon's delivery preferences.

What if I'm not home for delivery?

Carriers will attempt delivery 3 times before returning to sender. You can also authorize safe drop or hold at a pickup location through the carrier's website.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Gift wrapping is available for many items through Amazon at checkout. Options include gift boxes, bags, and personalized messages.

Contact Us
